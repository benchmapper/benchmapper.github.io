Getting started with OpenStreetMap

Create an account at OpenStreetMap.org. If you're adding benches near your home, you may wish to pick a pseudonym.

Don't use spaces in your account name. Not all OpenStreetMap editors support spaces.

Desktop/Laptop

If you're using a desktop or laptop computer, you can use the editor on OpenStreetMap.org.

Mobile

On mobile, you can use a dedicated OpenStreetMap editor app.

iPhone/iPad

The best OpenStreetMap editor for iPhone is "Go Map!!", spelled with two exclamation marks.

Download Go Map!! from the Apple iOS App Store.

Android

The most popular OpenStreetMap editor for Android is Vespucci. You can download Vespucci from the Google Play store or on the Vespucci website.

Making changes

Once you've approved changes, you still have to save them to the OpenStreetMap server.

Desktop/Laptop

Click the "OSM" button on the left sidebar to open the OpenStreetMap editor, at your current location.

Mobile

Click the "pencil" icon open your phone's OpenStreetMap editor. (If you don't have one installed, the button won't do anything.)
